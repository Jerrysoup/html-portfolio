I've had the opportunity to conceptualize, code, and design games from the ground up, honing my skills in 2D and 3D game development, game mechanics, and level design. Self-teaching has provided me with the autonomy to experiment and innovate in the world of coding and game development, and it continues to be an exhilarating journey filled with limitless possibilities.
